Welcome

PWAs are web applications that look and feel like native applications. They are fast, reliable, and engaging. Users can install them to their home screen, receive web push notifications, and work offline.

They load instantly, even on flaky networks, and never show the downasaur, even in uncertain connectivity. Service workers allow them to work offline, or on low quality networks. And, most importantly, they are reliable, and re-engageable: even if they start offline, they keep working and re-load the last viewed screen when back on-line.
